FastCast

Mission Statement

FastCast is a search engine that aims to provide people around the world whom have busy, dynamic, and perhaps non-lenient schedules with podcasts that are time and content relevent.

Motivation

Have you ever had a ten-minute break and wished you could enjoy listening to a podcast on the fly--without having to endlessly search through your countless libraries only to find out that you have already wasted five minutes? Sure, you can listen to a fifty-minute podcast and pause ten minutes in, but we believe there is instrinsic value in listening to a podcast without interruption. Would you enjoy watching an hour-long movie in increments of ten minutes? Probably not. Would you have a richer understanding of that movie if you had watched it that way? Again, probably not.

Just Cut to the Chase

FastCast is here to provide you with high-end podcasts that you can enjoy from begining-to-end no matter how short, or long, your break permits you.

The creators of FastCast

Because we respect your time, we have dedicated countless hours perfecting a service we know you will not only enjoy but one that you will appreciate beyond any quantifiable measure. Although our brand might be strictly, and mistakenly, categorized as entertainment, we encourage our users to think of FastCast as a lifestyle app; one that will augment your day-to-day life with convenience.
